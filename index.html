Summer Holiday

10000 Roses Cafe & More

Roses Cafe & More is a cozy cafe located in Cebu, Philippines. With a wide selection of delicious food options, from all-day breakfast, specialty coffee, and international dishes, Roses Cafe & More has something for everyone. They offer a relaxed atmosphere and a friendly, welcoming staff. Stop by for a bite to eat, or just to hang out with friends and enjoy the view of the city.

Onok Island

Onok Island is located in the Balabac group of islands in Palawan, Philippines. It is a small island, just over one mile in length and a few hundred feet wide. It is surrounded by turquoise waters and is surrounded by coral reefs and mangrove swamps. Onok Island is a great place for snorkeling, swimming, and kayaking. It is also great for bird watching and for just relaxing in the sun.

Coron Island

Coron Island in Palawan is a paradise of crystal clear waters, white sand beaches, and stunning limestone cliffs. It is a perfect destination for snorkeling, diving, and exploring the many hidden coves and lagoons. There are also plenty of activities to do such as kayaking, hiking, and island hopping. Whether you want to relax or explore, Coron Island is the perfect destination.

Amanpulo Beach Resort

Amanpulo Beach Resort is a luxurious private island resort located in the Philippines. It features private villas, white sand beaches, lush tropical gardens and a host of amenities, including a private spa, restaurants, a pool, and a dive centre. Guests can enjoy a variety of activities such as kayaking, snorkelling, diving, sailing and more, while taking in the breathtaking views of the archipelago.

Boracay Beach Resort

Boracay Beach Resort is a luxurious 4-star beachfront resort located on Boracay Island, Philippines. Guests can enjoy a wide range of modern amenities, such as a large pool, spa, restaurant, bar, and activities like kayaking, snorkelling, and windsurfing. The resort also offers a variety of accommodation options, from stylish and contemporary rooms to luxurious villas.

Siargao Island

Siargao Island is an idyllic tropical paradise located off the coast of Palawan in the Philippines. It boasts stunning beaches, crystal clear waters, lush jungles, and a laid-back atmosphere. It's a great destination for surfers, divers, beach bums, and nature lovers.
